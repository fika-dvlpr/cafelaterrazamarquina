Our Coffee Shop

Cafe La Terraza Marquina is a charming local café located in Marikina City that has won the hearts of coffee enthusiasts with its artisanal brews and inviting ambiance. Nestled in a cozy corner, this hidden gem exudes a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making it the perfect retreat for coffee lovers seeking solace or a casual catch-up with friends.

The aroma of freshly ground coffee fills the air, enticing passersby to step in and savor the rich flavors meticulously crafted by their skilled baristas. With its focus on providing a delightful coffee experience.

We offer delicious and reasonably priced food and beverages.
